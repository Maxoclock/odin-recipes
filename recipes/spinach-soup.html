Spinach Soup

An easy soup!

This soup comes together with minimal fuss and is a quick way to get vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients:

  • Large bag of fresh spinach (or block of frozen)
  • Two russet potatoes
  • One large yellow onion
  • One cube bouillon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sour cream for garnish

Steps

  1. Chop and sautee onion til soft.
  2. Add cut potatoes and cover with water.
  3. Simmer until potatoes are soft.
  4. Wilt spinach in liquid, blend with hand blender, and season to taste. Garnish with sour cream.
