Crockpot Beans

Cooking beans from dried using a crockpot is the easiest!

By cooking our beans in a crockpot, we can skip the hours of soaking. Please note that some people who react negatively to beans (gas, etc) may find this method exacerbates their issues.

If you're like me, this isn't a problem. We can cook our beans in the crockpot and use them in future recipes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried beans (ex: navy)
  • 2 cups water
  • Large pinch of salt
  • Flavourings: smoked pork, bay leaves, onion (optional)

Steps:

  1. Place all ingredients in crockpot. Recipe can be scaled up depending on how much you need to cook.
  2. Cook on low until beans are soft and creamy inside.
